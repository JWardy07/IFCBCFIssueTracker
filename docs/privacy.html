Privacy Policy

App: IFC BCF Issue Tracker

Last updated: 23 April 2026

IFC BCF Issue Tracker respects your privacy. The app is designed to work locally with BIM Collaboration Format files that you choose to import.

Data Collection

IFC BCF Issue Tracker does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Files and Project Data

BCF files, comments, snapshots, issue details, and exported reports remain on your device unless you choose to share or export them using system sharing features.

Analytics and Tracking

The app does not use analytics, advertising identifiers, third-party tracking, or behavioral profiling.

Network Access

The core app functionality does not require an account or online service. External links may open related App Store pages if you choose to tap them.
